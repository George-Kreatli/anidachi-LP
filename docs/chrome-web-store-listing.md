## Detailed Description

### Watch Anime Together with AniDachi

AniDachi is a Chrome extension for anime fans who want to watch Crunchyroll together with friends. Create shared watchrooms, sync playback, and chat in real-time — or watch at your own pace with async group watching.

**Why AniDachi?**

- **Auto Anime Detection:** Navigate to any anime on Crunchyroll and AniDachi instantly identifies the show, season, and episode. No manual URL sharing.
- **One-Click Watchrooms:** Create a shared watchroom and invite friends with a single link. Everyone watches in sync.
- **Asynchronous Watching:** Friends don't need to be online at the same time. Watch at your own pace, mark episodes, and leave reactions for friends to see later.
- **Real-Time Chat:** Discuss plot twists, share theories, and react to every moment with built-in episode chat.
- **Progress Tracking:** Track which episodes everyone has watched. No more "wait, which episode are you on?" confusion.

**How It Works:**

1. Install AniDachi from the Chrome Web Store.
2. Navigate to any anime on Crunchyroll.
3. Click "Detect Anime" — AniDachi identifies the show automatically.
4. Create a watchroom and share the invite link with friends.
5. Watch together with synced playback, or watch asynchronously and leave reactions.

**Perfect for:**

- Long-distance anime watch parties
- Friend groups across different time zones
- Ongoing series where everyone watches at different speeds
- Anime clubs and community watchalongs

**Supported Platforms:** Crunchyroll (more platforms coming soon)

**Requirements:** Each person needs their own Crunchyroll account. AniDachi subscription starts at $8/month with a full refund guarantee.

AniDachi (アニ友) means "anime friend" in Japanese. Built by anime fans, for anime fans.
